Campaign Flights

Five stories, one wave

Rather than booking creators individually, we propose organizing the campaign into thematic flights. Each flight tells a distinct story while building toward a collective "Brand Family" wave across Q2 and Q3. Creators can appear in multiple flights.

01

"The NAD+ Effect": Science Innovation Launch

NAD+ Mist

Position the campaign at the intersection of longevity science and K-beauty. Creators explain NAD+ to their audiences: the same molecule celebrities pay $500+ per IV drip session for, now in a $22 mist. Lead with the science, land with the product.

"Hailey Bieber spends $500 on NAD+ drips. This K-beauty brand put it in a $22 mist."
Lead creators: Miki Rai (RN credibility) • Michelle Choi (lifestyle authority) • Lena Yeo (wellness bridge)
02

"Target Run Treasure": Retail Discovery

Barrier Essence

The organic Target discovery moment. Creator finds haruharu wonder on the K-beauty shelf, tries it, and it becomes a staple. "Come shopping with me" format drives foot traffic and makes the purchase moment feel like an adventure, not an ad.

"Found this award-winning K-beauty essence at Target for under $30 and my skin has never been this hydrated."
Lead creators: Heart Defensor (lifestyle + beauty) • Erin Dugan Jurchak (TikTok reach) • Hope Gainer (high engagement)
03

"Day by Day": The Collective Ritual

Both Products

"haruharu" means "day by day" in Korean. Build a collective content series where each creator shows their daily ritual incorporating both products. Morning: Barrier Essence as hydration base. Midday: NAD+ Mist refresh. Creates unified campaign aesthetic while allowing individual storytelling.

"Day by day, your skin gets better. Here's my haruharu ritual."
All 12 creators • Shared hashtag: #myharuharuritual • Staggered rollout per timeline
